Self-driven growth runs through my career: BIM and multidisciplinary coordination from undergraduate study; visual programming and generative design at master's level — turning business rules into testable workflows. That thread continues — I engage with digitisation through domain knowledge and hands-on work, rather than waiting for ready-made tools.

At a digital transformation workshop organised by my department in 2024, I framed this further: be your own product manager first — clarify what you need and why — so pain points on the job can more efficiently become tools people actually use. In the AI era, Vibe Coding has been my way of testing that path: shipping lightweight tools with Cursor despite no programming background, across document control, procurement, and case-library workflows.

I also bring 4+ years managing fit-out projects end-to-end, with involvement in enterprise AI adoption. Seeking to combine engineering depth, sustained digital exploration, and product thinking to drive practical, reusable upgrade in AEC.
